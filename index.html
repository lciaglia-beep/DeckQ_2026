QANTICA

Technology at the service of the human creative process

Qantica is a technology company that deploys AI and other tools under the guidance of an experienced media team. Our goal is to partner with creators to empower them and help them maximize the value of their intellectual properties (IPs) using specifically designed tools.

Market Context

The Problem

98%

Failure Rate

Through conversations with many prominent industry people, we realized that the IP/creative process universe is in crisis. Around 98% of the movie projects approved, fail.

Decreasing Quality

Quality is decreasing, and as a famous producer told us, there is "remote control fatigue".

The Neglected Core

We believe that creators are the neglected core of the entertainment industry (media, TV, films, games).

Threatened by AI

AI is emerging as a massive disruption, generating uncertainty and putting traditional creative processes and job security at extreme risk.

Value Proposition

The Solution

Human-Centric Approach

Through a Socratic Method of structured questions, we extract the human soul and vision of the creator to empower their IP, ensuring the core of entertainment remains authentically human.

AI Integration

AI is utilized not as a replacement, but as an advanced tool to assist in the curation and sophisticated development input of the creator's original vision, crossing boundaries from films to games.

Blockchain

Blockchain operates over the patent and the operating system leading processes on legitimacy, traceability, and immutability, effectively avoiding costly IP disputes.

Timing

Why Now?

There is turbulence at the intersection of streaming services, gaming companies, and content creation, much of it prompted by recent technological developments and audience frustrations. AI is seen as both an opportunity and a massive threat.

These facts prompted us to imagine how the industry would have started today with all currently available resources, instead of being constrained based on the way Hollywood was developed more than a century ago.

"Based on this dynamic, Qantica proposes a path: working with our patent involves content from the artist that is reflected in a blockchain contract."
