QANTICA

Technology at the service of the creative process

Qantica is a technology company that deploys AI and other tools under the guidance of an experienced media team. Our goal is to partner with creators to empower them and help them maximize the value of their intellectual properties (IPs) using specifically designed tools.

Market Context

The Problem

98%

Failure Rate

Through conversations with many prominent industry people, we realized that the IP/creative process universe is in crisis. Around 98% of the movie projects approved, fail.

Decreasing Quality

Quality is decreasing, and as a famous producer told us, there is "remote control fatigue".

The Neglected Core

We believe that creators are the neglected core of the entertainment industry (media, TV, films, games).

Value Proposition

The Solution

Qantica's Smart System

Qantica has developed a technology that allows it to curate, develop and sell IPs maximizing their value by exploring all available distribution channels, from movies to games. At the core is QIP, our US patented proprietary method.

Empowering Creators

We help creators get proper recognition. By giving them a cutting edge method, gaming possibilities, sophisticated development input, a dedicated audience and a targeted auction process, creators extract a larger share. Creators are true bearers of vision.

AI & Blockchain

Qantica uses both AI and Blockchain to provide a robust infrastructure through QSS. It partners with creators, avoiding IP disputes. It is the only startup that works with both films/TV and games.

Timing

Why Now?

There has been an increasing focus at the turbulent intersection of streaming services, gaming companies, and content creation, much of it prompted by recent technological developments and audience frustrations.

Recent technological development prompted us to imagine how the industry would have started today with all currently available resources, instead of being constrained based on the way Hollywood was developed more than a century ago.

"We believe Qantica could be an answer. It represents the revolutionary smart contract of the creative process. The crucial link that completes the IP chain and enhances its quality and market value."
