QANTICA

Technology at the service of the human creative process

Qantica is a technology company that deploys AI and other tools under the guidance of an experienced media team. Our goal is to partner with creators to empower them and help them maximize the value of their intellectual properties (IPs) using tailor-made tools.

The Problem

98%

Failure Rate

Through conversations with many prominent industry people, we realized that the IP/creative process universe is in crisis. Around 98% of the movie projects approved, fail.

Decreasing Quality

Quality is decreasing, and as a famous producer told us, there is "remote control fatigue".

The Neglected Core

We believe that creators are the neglected core of the entertainment industry (media, TV, films, games).

Threatened by AI

AI is emerging as a massive disruption, generating uncertainty and putting traditional creative processes and job security at risk.

The Solution

Human-Centered Approach

Through a Socratic Method of structured questions, we extract the human soul and vision of the creator to empower their IP, ensuring the core of entertainment remains authentically human.

AI Integration

AI is utilized not as a replacement, but as an advanced patented tool that supports the curation and sophisticated development of the creator’s original vision, eliminating boundaries between films and games.

Blockchain

Blockchain operates through the patented process and operating system leading to legitimacy, traceability, and immutability. It effectively avoids costly IP disputes.

The Technology

QSS

Qantica's Smart System

Works via 3 interconnected levels to curate, develop and sell IPs. Maximizes value through the exploration of all available distribution channels, from movies to games.

Why Now?

There is growing turbulence at the intersection of streaming services, gaming companies, and content creation, driven by recent technological developments and audience frustrations. AI is seen as both an opportunity and a significant threat.

These facts prompted us to imagine how the industry would have started today with all currently available resources, instead of being constrained based on the way Hollywood was developed more than a century ago.

Qantica proposes a new path. Partnering with writers, and giving them the tools needed to take their proper place in industry revenue allocation.
